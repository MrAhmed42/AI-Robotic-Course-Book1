# The Course Details

This is a comprehensive, hands-on course designed for students and professionals looking to work at the intersection of AI and robotics. The focus is on **Physical AI**â€”intelligence that is trained in and operates within a physical environment.

### Course Goals

The primary goal is to provide a complete, end-to-end framework for building a simulated autonomous humanoid robot capable of understanding and executing high-level natural language commands.

### Prerequisites

* Proficiency in Python and basic C++ is required.
* Familiarity with Linux (Ubuntu) terminal commands.
* Prior exposure to machine learning frameworks (PyTorch/TensorFlow) is helpful but not mandatory.
